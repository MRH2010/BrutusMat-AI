About BrutusMat AI

BrutusMat AI is a human-guided artificial intelligence platform designed to support research, digital marketing training, professional writing, project management, and capacity building. It operates under strict human supervision and is intended as a support tool—not a replacement for human judgment or institutional expertise.

Core Capabilities

  • Digital marketing training and content planning
  • CV, thesis, and professional document writing
  • Project management, MEL guidance, and reporting
  • Learning support and skills development
